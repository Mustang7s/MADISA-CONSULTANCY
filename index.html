About Us

Madisa Consulting is a Jubbaland-registered consultancy (License No. 1910) specializing in conflict-sensitive operations across the Horn of Africa since 2015. Certified by the Jubbaland Security Agency (Ref: HSNJ/HDQ/01 1032/25), we deliver ISO-standard third-party monitoring, security advisories, and humanitarian program implementation to empower resilience in WASH, GBV prevention, and livelihood initiatives.

Ensuring safety and resilience through expert risk management, crisis response, and strategic security solutions.

Through culturally-grounded expertise, transparent third-party assessments, and rigorous safety protocols, we empower NGOs, governments, and communities to achieve measurable impact in high-risk environments..

Committed to Somali-led solutions, we adhere to Jubbaland’s regulatory standards while integrating global best practices in WASH, GBV prevention, and livelihood resilience for lasting, community-driven success.

At MADISA Consultancy, we deliver impact-driven solutions in livelihoods, humanitarian aid, governance, peacebuilding, and research, empowering organizations
